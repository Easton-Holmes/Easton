Beta Fish

Caretaking Instructions

Beta Fish need a tank that is at least 3 gallons in size. My roomate and I
got a 3.5 gallon tank for our fish. Beta fish are very agresive, and they
cannot share a tank with another beta fish. They will also attack themselves
if they see their reflection, so you can't set them up near a mirrior without
some kind of barrier in between. There are some different species of fish that
they can share a tank with, and they are safe with snails.

Beta fish are tropical fish and they need warm water in order to survive. I got a water
heater for my fish from Amazon

Fish need their water to be conditioned before you put them in a tank. Every
time you clean the tank you need to add water conditioner and bacteria starter
to the tank. Fish also need food. My roomate and I bought flakes and pebbels
for our fish, but beta fish have very small mouhes and he can only eat the flakes.

Decorations

It is not nessecary for fish to have decorations in their tank, but it deffinetly makes
them happier. For our fish, my roomate and I got pebbels, fake plants, a cave, and a
background paper.
